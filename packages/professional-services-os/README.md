# SBB Professional ServicesOS Foundation (BOSF-030)

The Professional Services Operating System (ProfessionalServicesOS) is SBB's core industry-specific operating system designed to orchestrate the complete client engagement lifecycle, manage client accounts and service agreements, track proposals and statements of work, monitor project deliverables and milestones, assign and allocate consultants based on skills and availability, record timesheets and expense entries, manage billing requests and revenue recognition, track customer success health scores, and deliver professional services intelligence under human-supervised AI guidance.

## Modules Included

* **core/**: Services framework context, engagement lifecycles, and main ProfessionalServicesFramework contract.
* **clients/**: Client profiles, client accounts, engagement tracking, and multi-tier service agreements.
* **proposals/**: Formal proposals, legally binding statements of work, and project quotations.
* **projects/**: Client projects, project milestones, deliverables, and structured work packages.
* **resources/**: Consultant registries, skills matrices, resource allocations, and target utilization metrics.
* **delivery/**: Client service requests, structured work logs, task assignments, and quality review audits.
* **time/**: Weekly timesheets, billable hours, and expense entry tracking.
* **billing/**: Detailed invoice requests, billing milestone triggers, and complex revenue recognition schedules.
* **customer-success/**: Customer health indicators, client renewal pipelines, and customer satisfaction logs.
* **analytics/**: Operational professional services dashboards, detailed reports, and core KPIs.
* **ai/**: Future resource optimization, project risk analysis, utilization diagnostics, and customer health predictions.
* **events/**: Business-level events (engagement created, proposal approved, project started, milestone completed, invoice requested, engagement closed).
