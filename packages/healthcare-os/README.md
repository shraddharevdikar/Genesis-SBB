# SBB HealthcareOS Foundation (BOSF-024)

The Healthcare Operating System (HealthcareOS) is SBB's industry-specific operating system designed to orchestrate patients, healthcare providers, appointments, clinical workflows (encounters, diagnoses, care plans), pharmacy services, laboratory systems, facility bed/ward operations, medical billing, insurance processes, and clinical decision support analysis by composing existing SBB Operating Systems.

## Modules Included

* **core/**: Healthcare execution context, clinical care lifecycles, and HealthcareFramework contract interface.
* **patients/**: Patient registries, verified identifiers, comprehensive profiles, and emergency contacts.
* **providers/**: Physician directories, nurse classifications, medical specialists, and credentials.
* **appointments/**: Appointment schedulers, queue waiting lists, and schedule intervals.
* **clinical/**: Medical encounters, primary diagnoses, medical care plans, treatment plans, and clinical notes.
* **pharmacy/**: Medication catalogs, drug prescriptions, and administration tracking.
* **laboratory/**: Diagnostic tests, laboratory orders, and verified lab results.
* **facilities/**: Ward locations, beds allocations, and department capacity parameters.
* **billing/**: Healthcare billing accounts, insurance claims, and payment authorizations.
* **analytics/**: Hospital dashboards, medical performance reports, and quality of care KPIs.
* **ai/**: Clinical decision support, patient risk models, capacity forecasters, and resource optimizers.
* **events/**: Healthcare domain events for patient admission, booking, diagnosis, prescription, and discharge.
