# SBB RealEstateOS Foundation (BOSF-023)

The Real Estate Operating System (RealEstateOS) is SBB's core industry-specific operating system designed to orchestrate property lifecycles, track land acquisitions, manage complex tower/phase construction structures, control unified buyer/channel partner sales, and direct community property management operations by composing existing SBB Operating Systems.

## Modules Included

* **core/**: Real Estate context, property lifecycle transitions, and RealEstateFramework contract interface.
* **land/**: Land parcel profiles, acquisition transactions, and zoning approvals.
* **projects/**: Real Estate multi-tower projects, development phases, and structured unit inventory logs.
* **crm/**: Buyer registries, broker networks, and channel partner parameters.
* **sales/**: Booking details, dynamic unit allocations, and milestone-linked payment schedules.
* **construction/**: Construction stage tasks, physical milestone assessments, contractors, and quality inspections.
* **handover/**: Possession registries, snag/punch list trackers, and structure warranties.
* **property-management/**: Society boards, resident directories, and maintenance requests.
* **analytics/**: Real Estate dashboards, custom project reports, and critical project performance KPIs.
* **ai/**: AI agents, demand forecasting, automated pricing optimizers, and project risk predictors.
* **events/**: Decoupled real estate domain events streamed on major property lifecycle occurrences.
