# SBB LegalOS Foundation (BOSF-021)

The Legal Operating System (LegalOS) Foundation module defines SBB's core domain-specific structures, contract lifecycle matrices, compliance frameworks, corporate governance board controls, legal litigation logs, intellectual property registries, enterprise audit recordings, multi-tier legal approvals, and continuous compliance AI-led feedback loops.

## Modules Included

* **core/**: Legal lifecycle, context, and LegalFramework contract interface.
* **contracts/**: Contract templates, signing parties, active obligations, and renewal mechanisms.
* **compliance/**: Auditable compliance requirements, internal control metrics, and regulatory checks.
* **governance/**: Delegated authority limits, board resolutions, and periodic governance compliance audits.
* **risk/**: Legal risk assessments, regulatory risk scores, litigation case tracking, and active dispute filings.
* **intellectual-property/**: Registered patents, trade secrets, active copyrights, and trademark filings.
* **approvals/**: Multi-stage legal review and approval records with decision matrix triggers.
* **audit/**: Legal audits, findings, corrective and preventive action (CAPA) tracking.
* **analytics/**: Legal performance dashboards, regulatory compliance reports, and active risk KPIs.
* **ai/**: Compliance scanning engines, contract risk predictors, and regulatory warning systems.
* **events/**: Enterprise domain events dispatched on key legal lifecycle stage changes.
