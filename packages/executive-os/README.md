# SBB ExecutiveOS Foundation (BOSF-022)

The Executive Operating System (ExecutiveOS) is the highest-level operating system in the Sovereign Business Brain (SBB) platform. It orchestrates and synthesizes intelligence, risk governance, performance metrics, and portfolio optimization across all other operating systems (MarketingOS, SalesOS, FinanceOS, HROS, OperationsOS, and LegalOS).

## Key Capabilities Included

* **core/**: Core framework contracts, executive context schemas, and unified Enterprise Command Center definitions.
* **strategy/**: Multi-year strategic plans, structured enterprise objectives (OKRs), strategic initiatives, and cross-functional corporate transformation programs.
* **performance/**: Dynamic enterprise scorecards, balanced scorecard matrices, executive performance reviews, and executive KPI indices.
* **portfolio/**: Unified enterprise investment portfolios, capability mapping registries, and initiative prioritization matrices.
* **governance/**: High-level executive decision logs, board meetings, and board delegation of authority limit structures.
* **risk/**: Strategic risk models, comprehensive enterprise risk registers, and risk mitigation action plan tracking.
* **intelligence/**: Executive briefings, recommendations, real-time enterprise health alerts, and synthesized corporate insights.
* **forecasting/**: Financial/strategic planning cycles, multi-variable scenario analyses, and enterprise forecasts.
* **analytics/**: Executive dashboards, executive reports, and enterprise KPIs.
* **ai/**: Strategic AI advisors, predictive insights, and automated enterprise health analysis.
* **events/**: Decoupled domain events emitted during high-level strategic changes and executive milestones.
